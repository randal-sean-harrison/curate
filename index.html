manage

Need to curate and elevate your entire portfolio of work?

  • Working with a team?
  • Need a data management plan?
  • Need to regulate levels of access
  • Working with supplemental materials?
  • Working with multiple formats?

CurateND is a research portal that manages research portfolios on various levels. It offers streamlined consultation for creating data management plans to meet funding mandates. Upon deposit, you can control access privileges and embargo periods. CurateND accepts and preserves files of any format, including datasets, articles, images, videos, and presentations. You can deposit and showcase your portfolio and supplementary materials as desired.

preserve

Need to steward your research for the future?

  • Funding plan mandates a plan for long term preservation?
  • Need to locate your research and related work in future?
  • Need to preserve conference proceedings?

CurateND aims to preserve your work and that of your students for future study. CurateND follows preservation standards that fulfill funding agencies' requirements for long-term preservation and curation of research. Our preservation standards guarantee the security and accessibility of your work, regardless of grant funding. Additionally, the platform can migrate files to new formats for continued usability.

discover

Need a platform that optimizes sharing and discovery?

  • Need to illuminate your published research globally
  • Hoping to increase the amount your work is cited?
  • Want to share working papers, or negative results?

CurateND is an ideal solution for sharing other working papers and research results with the global research community, including multi-media and non-traditional publishing platforms. CurateND improves searchability and discoverability of publicly available content by using well-structured metadata and optimization. It also enables global access through a centralized platform for tracking downloads and citations at different levels.

share

Required to share your research, data, and related works?

  • Need to create a DOI for citing and sharing your data?
  • Want to highlight the work of graduate and undergraduate research?
  • Have images, posters, presentations, collections, white papers or datasets that you want to share?

CurateND implements front-end data management plans for grant-funded research and data sharing mandates. You can share content at different access levels, including restricted access, campus access, and open access. CurateND can also generate a DOI (Digital Object Identifier) on demand, making it easier for others to cite your work. Additionally, it supports multiple data formats and allows for the showcasing of undergraduate and graduate research contributions in all disciplines. Anyone from the campus community can contribute by creating an account.
